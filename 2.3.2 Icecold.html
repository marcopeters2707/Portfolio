Project: Icecold

Introduction

In order to test out Qlik Sense, one of the leading business intelligence solutions on the Norwegian market, I created a dashboard for a virtual ice cream manufacturer. The manufacturer, "Icecold", supplies ice cream and other frozen products to companies and households in South-Eastern Norway. The data is also fictitious and consists of sales data as well as data on customers, employees, products and markets.

Dashboard

To visualize the data, I created a simple interactive one-page dashboard. Here, I included date-based filters and filters on county, municipality and teams. The dashboard contains KPIs on sales, costs, profit and more. Sales and profit over time is visualized in a line diagram which clearly indicates that sales and profit vary with the seasons. The bar charts visualize sales and other KPIs per customer and product, while pie charts are used to provide a quick representation of the relative size (in sales) of various product categories.
