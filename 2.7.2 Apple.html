Project: Apple

Introduction

In the Maven Environmental Challenge, a data challenge hosted by Maven Analytics, the objective was to analyze data from Apple's yearly updated Environmental Progress report. In this year's report, Apple announced major progress towards their goal of carbon neutrality by 2030. But does the data show the same progress?

In my submission I built this interactive power BI report which, based on the provided historical data, projects different paths towards carbon neutrality. I focused on how much gross greenhouse gas emissions have been reduced, both in absolute and relative terms, and which average yearly reduction rate is needed for Apple to achieve its objective.
